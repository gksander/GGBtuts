This site is comprised of resources for learning GeoGebra, and ideas for using GeoGebra in teaching scenarios. These resources were put together by Grant Sander -- you can contact him at gksander93@gmail.com.

GeoGebra is a graphing calculator for functions, geometry, algebra, calculus, statistics and 3D math. You can find more at geogebra.org, or begin navigating this site to find out more.
