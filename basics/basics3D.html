3D Basics

On this page, we will look at some of GeoGebra's 3D functionalities. With GeoGebra, you can easily define 3D points, segments, conics, curves, surfaces and more! You can also have GeoGebra's 3D view open while you have a 2D graphics view open -- and these two views can interact!
